Privacy

Rabbit Search is a privacy-focused meta search engine that does not track or store any of your personal information or search queries. We believe that your search history and personal information should remain private, and we take great care to ensure that this is the case.

Open Source

Rabbit Search is open source software licensed under the GPL-3.0 license, which means that anyone can access, modify, and distribute the source code. This promotes transparency and allows developers to contribute to the project, making it more secure and reliable.

Customization

Rabbit Search includes multiple themes, which means that you can customize the look and feel of the search engine to suit your preferences. Whether you prefer a dark or light theme, or something in between, Rabbit Search has you covered.
